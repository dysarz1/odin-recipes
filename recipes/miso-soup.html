Miso Soup

Ingredents

  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, combine dashi granules and water; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium,and whisk in the miso paste.
  3. Stir in tofu. Separate the layers of the green onions, and add them to the soup.
  4. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
